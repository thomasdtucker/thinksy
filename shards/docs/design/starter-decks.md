# Starter Decks

## Deck 1: Blazebloom Surge (Aggressive Pressure)

**Strategy:** Play creatures early. Trigger Fractures to push damage. Win before opponent stabilizes.

### Avatar: Kael, Ember Binder
- HP: 12
- Passive: First Fracture each turn deals +1 damage
- Ultimate (charge 5): Deal 3 damage anywhere

|------|------|------|--------|-----|
| Shard Shock | 2 | Impact | Deal 2 to a unit (3 with Kael passive) | 4 |
| Nexus Surge | 3 | Impact | Deal 2 to enemy Nexus | 2 |
| Zero Gravity | 3 | Environmental | Units cannot block this turn | 2 |

### Relics (6)

| Card | Effect | Qty |
|------|--------|-----|

Play early units > Shard Shock to clear blockers > Zero Gravity to swing through > Nexus Surge + Avatar Ultimate to finish. "Attack and destabilize."

**Strategy:** Survive early. Draw cards. Let Fractures reset the board. Win late with big threats.

## Simulated Match: Blazebloom vs Tidal

### Turn 1
- **Blaze:** Plays Verdant Initiate (1/2)
- **Tidal:** Passes (no 1-cost card)

### Turn 2
- **Blaze:** Plays Ember Scout (2/1, deals 1). Attacks with Initiate.
- **Tidal HP:** 14 -> 12
- **Tidal:** Plays Tidal Guard (1/4 wall). Defense begins.

### Turn 3
- **Blaze:** Uses Shard Shock (3 damage with passive). Guard now at 1 HP. Attacks, kills it.
- **Tidal HP:** 12 -> 10
- **Tidal:** Plays Wave Adept, draws 1 card. Stabilizing.

### Turn 4
- **Blaze:** Plays Flame Warden. Uses Zero Gravity (units cannot block). Attacks for 6.
- **Tidal HP:** 10 -> 4
- **Tidal:** Uses Rift Collapse (3 to all units). Board cleared.

### Turn 5
- **Blaze:** Plays Blazebloom Warden (5/5)
- **Tidal:** Uses Dimensional Pause. Removes it for one turn.

### Turn 6
- **Blaze:** Replays Warden. Uses Nexus Surge (3 damage with passive). Activates Ultimate (3 damage).
- **Tidal HP:** 4 -> 0
- **Blaze wins.**

### Match Takeaway
Blazebloom teaches attacking, damage, aggressive tempo. Tidal teaches defense, card draw, board control. They feel different but balanced. The core game is simple: Energy -> Play cards -> Attack -> Win. Everything else (Ships, Harnesses, Relics, Fusion) are modifiers on top.

## How Ships & Harnesses Would Affect This Match

If Aggro had Ember Dreadnought + Chrono Harness:
- Spark Bolt might deal 4 instead of 3

If Control had Tidal Observatory + Gravitas Harness:
- Rift Collapse might cost less or reduce more damage

Layers add depth without changing basic rules.
