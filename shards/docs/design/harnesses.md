# Nexus Harnesses (Exo-Suit System)

The Nexus is unstable. Binders must wear adaptive armor to survive its distortions.

## Properties
- Extremely rare (rarest items in game)
- Equipped to your Avatar
- NOT part of your 30-card deck
- Permanent account unlocks
- Harder to get than Ship Parts
- Mechanical, not just cosmetic

## How Harnesses Work

Each player may equip:
- **1 Primary Harness**
- **0-2 Micro-Mods** (rare augment attachments)

Harnesses modify:
- Avatar ability
- Energy mechanics
- Movement/tempo mechanics
- Damage mitigation
- Mutation stability

**Ships modify the battlefield. Harnesses modify the Binder.**

## The 5 Core Harness Types

### 1. Chrono-Flux Harness (Speed/Tempo)
- First card each turn costs 1 less
- Can act twice with one unit once per match
- Slightly reduced max HP
- **Playstyle:** Aggressive tempo players

### 2. Gravitas Bulwark Suit (Defense/Sustain)
- First damage to Nexus each turn reduced by 2
- Gain shield when healing exceeds max
- Slightly slower energy ramp
- **Playstyle:** Control & survivability

### 3. Void-Touched Exoform (Mutation/Chaos)
- 10% chance for played cards to gain bonus effect
- 5% chance to backfire
- Mutations stronger, but unstable
- **Playstyle:** High variance, streamer chaos

### 4. Aether Interface Rig (Combo/Relic synergy)
- Relics trigger additional minor effects
- Can re-trigger one spell per match
- Units slightly lower base attack
- **Playstyle:** High-skill combo players

### 5. Stellar Vanguard Frame (Midrange/Power Scaling)
- Avatar gains +1 attack each time a unit evolves
- When your Nexus drops below 10, gain surge
- Slower early turns
- **Playstyle:** Late-game power spike

## Micro-Mod Attachments

Each Harness can equip **2 Micro-Mods**:

| Micro-Mod | Effect |
|-----------|--------|
| Energy Capacitor | Store 1 unused energy |
| Warp Stabilizer | Reduce mutation downside |
| Kinetic Booster | Units gain haste if alone |
| Shield Matrix | Shield when passing turn |

Slightly less rare than full Harnesses.
