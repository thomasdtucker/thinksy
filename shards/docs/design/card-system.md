# Card System

## Card Types

### 1. Avatar (1 per deck)
- Main hero
- HP: 10-15
- Passive ability
- Ultimate ability (charges over time)
- Think: Pokemon Trainer + Planeswalker hybrid

### 2. Units (Creatures)
- Cost Energy to play
- Have **Power** & **Guard**
- Can attack or defend
- Some evolve mid-match

### 3. Fractures (replaced Spells)
Temporary reality distortions. Not magic — instability.

**4 categories:**

| Type | Duration | Examples |
|------|----------|---------|
| Impact | Instant | Shard Shock (deal 2 to a unit), Energy Surge (gain 1 energy), Collapse (destroy lowest HP unit) |
| Environmental | 1-2 turns | Zero Gravity (units can't block), Echo Field (units trigger twice), Dark Eclipse (healing reversed) |
| Rift | Instant, risky | Corruption Pulse (buff random unit, could be enemy), Unstable Mirror (copy unit randomly), Wild Flux (all costs -1) |
| Persistent | 3 turns | Radiation Field (deal 1 to all units EOT), Accelerated Decay (units lose 1 HP/turn), Energy Feedback (unused energy deals damage) |

Starter decks use only Impact + Environmental. Rift Fractures are advanced.

### 4. Relics
- Ongoing passive effects
- Change game rules temporarily
- Seasonal relics rotate

### 5. Fusion Cards (Rare)
Two units combine into a stronger form mid-game. Scratches Pokemon evolution, Genshin synergy, WoW raid combo vibes.

## Energy System

Each turn:
- Gain **+1 Energy** (max 10)
- Play any combination of cards equal to your Energy
- Each Shard gives a different Energy bonus

## Shard Corruption Mechanic

Mid-game, unstable Shards can:
- Mutate your cards
- Randomly upgrade abilities
- Trigger unexpected rule changes

Adds chaos element and streamer excitement.

## Example Match Flow

| Turn | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Play Verdant Initiate (1/2 unit) |
| 3 | Drop Ember Mage, apply burn synergy |
| 5 | Fuse Verdant Initiate + Ember Mage into Blazebloom Warden |
| 7 | Opponent activates Void Corruption — your unit mutates (gains lifesteal, loses guard) |
| 9 | Trigger Avatar Ultimate "Nexus Reforge" — reset all relics, gain bonus energy |

Game swings dramatically.

## Why Fractures > Spells

- Spells = "I cast fireball" (generic fantasy)
- Fractures = "The battlefield tears open" (on-theme)
- Feels environmental, not magical
- Stronger branding and identity
- Works beautifully with Ships & Harnesses
